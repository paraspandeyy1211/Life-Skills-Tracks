# Grit and Growth Mindset — Assignment

## 1. Grit — Summary
Grit means having passion and perseverance towards long-term goals. It means not giving up easily even when things become difficult. Sometimes progress is slow, but with continuous effort and consistency, we can achieve success. 

---

## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset — Summary

Growth mindset means believing that our abilities and intelligence can improve with practice and learning. It means we should not think that we cannot do something. Instead, we should believe that we can learn and improve with effort. People with growth mindset accept challenges and learn from their mistakes.


---

## 3. Internal Locus of Control

What is Internal Locus of Control?  
Internal locus of control means believing that our success and failure mainly depends on our own effort, decisions, and actions, not on luck or external factors.

Key Point in the Video:  
To stay motivated, we should focus on things which we can control, such as our effort, habits, and attitude. Instead of blaming situations or other people, we should take responsibility and try to improve ourselves.

---

## 4. Key Points to Build a Growth Mindset

- Believe that skills can be developed with practice
- Accept and face challenges
- Learn from mistakes and feedback
- Stay persistent even after failure
- Focus on effort, not only results
- Keep learning new things
- Replace negative thoughts with learning attitude
- Accept feedback positively
- Step out of comfort zone
- Practice regularly 

---

## 5. My Action Ideas to Build Growth Mindset (Software Developer)

- Practice coding daily

- Track my progress weekly
